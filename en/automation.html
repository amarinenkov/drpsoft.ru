Problem

Everyone's busy. But processes move slower than they should.

On paper, work goes on all day. But a large share of the time goes not toward results, but toward hunting for the right file, retyping data, checking with colleagues and repeating the same operations.

Finding information

The policies, instructions, documents and data are all there — but employees struggle to find the right answer quickly.

Manual reports & Excel

Data is gathered by hand, copied between files, double-checked and consolidated into spreadsheets.

Repetitive documents

Emails, contracts, memos, reports and meeting notes are produced from the same templates over and over.

Approvals & waiting

Processes stall waiting on responses, statuses, clarifications and manual reminders.

Process

First we understand the process, then we roll out the tools.

First we look at how employees actually work and where time is lost. Then we collect the team's ideas, select the right tools and deploy them where the impact will be clear and measurable.

01

Study the current work

We look at the tasks, reports, spreadsheets, documents, systems and approvals employees deal with every day.

02

Identify routine work and time losses

We pinpoint where employees spend time searching, retyping data, preparing reports and waiting for answers.

03

Collect the team's ideas

We find out what employees themselves would like to automate: which tasks frustrate them, where they lack a knowledge base, templates or an assistant.

04

Select the AI tools

GigaChat, YandexGPT, enterprise AI assistants, knowledge base, report automation, Excel, document generation, integrations with 1C / ERP / CRM / BI.

05

Compare before / after

We measure how long the process took before rollout, what changed afterward and the impact the team gained.
